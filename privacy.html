Tideo — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Tideo (“the app”) is developed by Cronime Ltd. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. In short: your timers and settings never leave your device, and the only thing the app ever transmits is the text you choose to type into the optional AI feature — used solely to build your routine, never linked to you.

Summary

  • We do not require an account and do not collect your name, email, or any contact information.
  • The app shows no ads, contains no advertising or analytics SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.
  • Your timers, presets, events, reminders, and settings are stored only on your device (and in the app’s local App Group container so widgets and Live Activities can read them). We run no servers of our own and never receive this data.
  • The optional “Describe it” AI feature sends the text you type to Google (Gemini, via Firebase AI Logic) to generate your routine — with your consent, and nothing else is sent. See “AI features” below.

Data stored on your device

Timer configurations, presets, countdown events, reminders, onboarding state, daily-streak history, and preferences are saved locally on your device. We have no servers and no access to this data. Deleting the app deletes this data.

AI features (“Describe it”)

Tideo includes an optional feature that turns a typed phrase (for example “20 minute ab workout”) into timers, reminders, or countdowns. When you use it — and only after you accept a one-time consent notice — the text you typed is sent to Google’s Gemini service through Firebase AI Logic, solely to generate the requested routine. The result is shown to you for review before anything is created.

  • Only the text you type in that screen is transmitted. Your timers, reminders, events, stats, and settings are never sent.
  • There are no accounts, so the request is not linked to your identity, is not used for tracking, and is not used to build a profile of you.
  • Requests are processed by Google under the Google Privacy Policy.
  • If you never use “Describe it”, nothing is ever transmitted.

No advertising, no analytics, no tracking

Tideo contains no advertising network, no third-party analytics, and no tracking technologies. The app does not request the App Tracking Transparency permission and does not access your device’s advertising identifier.

Purchases

Tideo Pro is a one-time purchase processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Your purchase entitlement is verified on your device via Apple’s StoreKit.

Notifications and speech

Timer-completion notifications and spoken announcements are generated locally on your device. Notification content is not uploaded anywhere.

Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Your rights

We hold no personal data about you: everything the app stores lives on your device and is removed when you delete the app. Text sent to the optional AI feature is processed to generate your routine and is not retained by us (we have no servers); Google’s handling is described in its privacy policy linked above.

Changes

We will update this page if our data practices change. Material changes will be reflected in the effective date above.
